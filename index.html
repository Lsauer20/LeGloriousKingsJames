NBA Career

LeBron James entered the NBA in 2003 as the #1 overall pick by the Cleveland Cavaliers. Since then, he has gone on to have one of the most decorated and dominant careers in basketball history, playing for the Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers.

Across two decades, he has rewritten record books, won championships, and defined what it means to be a complete player on and off the court.
